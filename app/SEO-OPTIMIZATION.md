## On-Page SEO Implementation

### 1. Title Tags
- **Format**: Primary Keyword | Secondary Keyword - Brand Name
- **Length**: 50-60 characters
- **Example**: "Full-Stack Development NYC | React, Node.js - Coder Design"

### 2. Meta Descriptions
- **Length**: 150-160 characters
- **Include**: Location (New York, NYC, Manhattan), phone number, call-to-action
- **Example**: "Premier NYC development agency. Full-stack, AI & SEO services. Located at 17 State Street. Call (437) 239-2448 for free consultation."

### 3. Header Tags Hierarchy
```
H1: One per page, includes primary keyword + location
H2: Section headers with secondary keywords
H3: Sub-sections with long-tail keywords
```

### 4. Schema Markup (Structured Data)
✅ **Implemented**:
- Organization schema with address, phone, geo-coordinates
- LocalBusiness schema for NYC location
- Service schema for each offering
- Article schema for blog posts
- BreadcrumbList for navigation

### 5. Content Optimization
- **Keyword Density**: 1-2% for primary keywords
- **LSI Keywords**: Related terms naturally integrated
- **Word Count**: 800+ words per page minimum
- **Readability**: Flesch Reading Ease score 60+
